Detailed Analysis of the Capita Data Breach

Introduction

The Capita Data Breach in 2023 serves as a significant case study in cyber security, demonstrating the ongoing threat of data breaches and the importance of robust data protection measures.

What Happened

The breach became public in April 2023, with Capita acknowledging a potential data compromise. Key events included unauthorized system access and public admission of the breach on April 20, 2023.

Cause of the Incident

The breach was primarily caused by technical vulnerabilities and lapses in security protocols, highlighting a mix of technical and human factors.

Addressing the Vulnerability

Immediate actions included enhancing network security, patching vulnerabilities, and conducting audits to prevent future breaches.

Short- and Long-Term Damage

Short-term effects involved reputational damage and regulatory scrutiny. Long-term, Capita faced sustained reputation harm and the need for significant security investments.
